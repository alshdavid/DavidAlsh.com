Description:



This was one of those "did this really happen?" kind of things.

By this point, I had made two websites and thought that creating a start up was within my skill level. I mean, how hard could it be, right?

So a few friends and I sat down and started throwing around ideas. The idea of an "Uber for the adult industry" was floated, we all had a good laugh about it, but quickly changed the subject.

The next morning, curiosity drove me to contact third parties that would be interested in investing their time or brand in the project.

Within the day I found a business partner and the mixture of shock and traction set us off.

What started as a joke ended up in months of learning AngularJS, mixed with 18 hour 7 day weeks, coffee, coffee, sleeping on the floor, coffee and I still can't believe it happened.
