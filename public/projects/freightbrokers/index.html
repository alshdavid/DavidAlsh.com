Description:



The Freight Broker’s website was a good show of my skills with CSS animation and my attention to detail.

The client (and designer) had some vague ideas of what they wanted the animation to do. I was given the visual assets and given the run down.

The challenge here begins with making the truck look like it’s moving towards you, rather than simply zooming in and the challenge ends at making the whole scene responsive.

The background image is a great example of its issue being responsive. Every screen size has a different animation, and the animation remains performant, even on low end devices.



Features:


  • Responsive
  • Homepage slider.
  • Masonry Gallery
  • CSS animations.
  • JavaScript Animations.
