Brown Paper Bag

An incredible web development company, an incredible team.



I was hired by BrownPaperBag in 2016 as a front end developer.


During my time there, I worked in teams which put together websites and web applications for clients.


We formed a cohesive unit, where each member contributed their strengths where, between the throwing paper at each other and banter, we crafted high quality products.


In my time there, I produced a mojority of my professional work in my portfolio.

Due to how collaborative the team was, I was even given creative freedom to tackle projects in my own way which allowed me to introduce the team to Angular 2



While there I used the following technologies:


  • HTML/SCSS/JS/TS
  • PHP Laravel
  • WordPress
  • Angular 2
  • Node.js
